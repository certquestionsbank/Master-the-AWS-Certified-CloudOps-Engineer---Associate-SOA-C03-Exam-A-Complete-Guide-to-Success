The AWS Certified CloudOps Engineer - Associate certification - formerly known as AWS Certified SysOps Administrator - Associate - is designed for IT professionals responsible for managing and maintaining AWS workloads. This certification validates your ability to deploy, operate, and manage systems on AWS with reliability, security, and performance in mind.

## Why Earn the AWS Certified CloudOps Engineer - Associate Certification?

As organizations increasingly migrate workloads to AWS, the demand for skilled CloudOps engineers continues to rise. This certification demonstrates that you have the expertise to implement monitoring, automate deployments, ensure security compliance, and maintain high availability and disaster recovery strategies - all aligned with the AWS Well-Architected Framework.

## SOA-C03 Exam Overview

**Level:** Associate
**Format:** 65 questions (multiple choice or multiple response)
**Duration:** 130 minutes
**Cost:** 150 USD
**Languages:** English, Japanese, Korean, Simplified Chinese
**Passing Score:** 720

## Key Exam Domains

**Monitoring, Logging, Analysis, Remediation, and Performance Optimization**
Focus on tracking system health, analyzing performance metrics, and troubleshooting efficiently.

**Reliability and Business Continuity**
Learn to design resilient workloads, backup strategies, and disaster recovery procedures.

**Deployment, Provisioning, and Automation**
Test your skills with infrastructure automation using tools like AWS CloudFormation and the AWS CLI.

**Security and Compliance**
Understand how to apply IAM policies, encryption, and compliance controls.

**Networking and Content Delivery**
Apply networking fundamentals such as DNS, TCP/IP, and firewalls to AWS environments.

## Ideal Candidate

The target candidate should have:

- At least 1 year of hands-on experience with AWS deployment, operations, and troubleshooting.
- Experience as a system administrator or operations engineer managing hybrid or cloud environments.

## Preparation Tips

To succeed in this exam:

- Gain hands-on experience with AWS Management Console and AWS CLI.
- Review AWS whitepapers and Well-Architected Framework best practices.
- Practice scenario-based questions that focus on real-world operations and incident response.
